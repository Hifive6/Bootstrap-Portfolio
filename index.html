About Me

I grew up in the great state of Texas and attended the University of Texas El Paso. Go Miners! I received a Degree of Science in Psychology with Minor in Biology. The past 8 years I have been working for Cennox Inc, which is a subcontracting company with customers such as Bank of America, Wells Fargo and Tyco IS. My primary job is with Tyco IS conducting Fire Alarm inspection in commererial buildings. My personal interests include playing and watching basketball, playing video games, and spending time with my wife which is the greatest joy of my life. My primary goal coming into and out of this program is to learn skills to move forward my job skills, achieve better employment so I can spend more quality of time with my wife.
